Site Plan

Site Name

Kwekwe Chamber of commerce shall be the name of the site. Kwekwe is a city at the very center of Zimbabwe and that's where I grew up and is my family's city of residence

Site Purpose

The Kwekwe Chamber of Commerce eliminates the gap between the city Municipality, Business Environment and Social structures that exist among the different people and organizations that populate the city of Kwekwe.

Consider being more than just a resident but a contributing forerunner for those that will inherit this beautil geographic location. Understanding and taking part in regulating how the business and social ecosystem of Kwekwe stands is not the Municipality's role alone.Join us in taking charge of the future of Kwekwe for our sakes and for the benefit of our children.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: As a newly elected local government leader and councilor, I want to know what event the Kwekwe Chamber of Commerce is going to have that allows for communication between the City Council and local businesses.

Website Content Response: The Kwekwe Chamber of Commerce actively organizes regular town hall meetings where city council members and local business owners come together to discuss relevant topics. These events encourage open communication, collaboration, and foster a positive relationship between the City Council and the local business community. Visit our Events page to stay updated on upcoming town hall meetings and other networking opportunities.


Scenario 2: As a business owner, I am interested in knowing where I can find contact information for the chamber's board of directors.

Website Content Response: The contact information for the Kwekwe Chamber of Commerce's board of directors can be found on our website's Contact Us page. You will find the names, positions, and contact details of the board members listed there. We encourage you to reach out to them with any inquiries, feedback, or collaboration opportunities.


Scenario 3: As a potential investor or resident, I would like to know the population growth in the Kwekwe area.

Website Content Response: The Kwekwe Chamber of Commerce closely monitors the city's growth and provides relevant data to assist potential investors and residents. The most up-to-date information on population growth in the Kwekwe area can be found on our Demographics page. It includes historical data, current statistics, and projections, giving you valuable insights into the city's population trends.

Color Schema

I will use a Midnight-Green colour for the header and footer, Black for the navigation and Tiffany-Blue for my headings background.

These colors are relavant to Kwekwe as it is surrounded by rivers and green vegetation. They are also friendly to the visually impaired users as they as not so shouting and blend in well with the browser default white or black font colors.
